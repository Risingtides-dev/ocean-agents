[`LICENSE`](LICENSE). They do not grant permission to use Ocean names, logos,
wordmarks, application icons derived from the Ocean mark, or distinctive brand
assets in a way that suggests sponsorship, endorsement, or an official Rising

- refer to this project or an unmodified distribution;
- describe compatibility or interoperability;
- identify that your work is derived from Ocean; and
- reproduce factual attribution and legal notices.

Please choose a clearly different name and visual identity for modified
products, hosted services, or distributions that could otherwise be mistaken
for an official Ocean release. Do not remove required copyright, license, or
third-party attribution notices.

fair or nominative use. Questions about broader brand use can be directed to
