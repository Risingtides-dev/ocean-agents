# Contributing to Ocean Agents

Thank you for improving Ocean's reusable agent packages. Keep contributions
provider-agnostic and organization-neutral, preserve third-party notices, and
run the checks in [`AGENTS.md`](AGENTS.md) for every path you touch.

Unless you conspicuously state otherwise when submitting it, you license each
intentional contribution for inclusion in this repository under the project's
`MIT OR Apache-2.0` terms, at the recipient's option, without additional terms
or conditions. This does not override a separate written contribution agreement
or the license of third-party material.

Do not submit material you lack the right to contribute. Identify copied,
adapted, generated, or vendored work; preserve its source and notices; and update
[`docs/PROVENANCE.md`](docs/PROVENANCE.md) and
[`docs/provenance.json`](docs/provenance.json) when the release boundary changes.

Never contribute credentials, private destinations, production agent behavior,
company knowledge, campaigns, artists, private SOPs, or internal repository
paths. Contributions do not receive rights to Ocean names, logos, wordmarks, or
other protected brand assets beyond [`TRADEMARKS.md`](TRADEMARKS.md) and
applicable law.
